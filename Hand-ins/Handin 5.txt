6. Rasterization

param = (8 - 6) / (12 - 6) = 2/6 = 1/3

X.left = lerp(9, 3, 1/3)
= (1 - 1/3) * 9 + 1/3 * 3 = 6 + 1 = 7

param = (8 - 6) / (10 - 6) = 2/4 = 1/2
X.right = lerp(9, 11, 1/2)
= (1 - 1/2) * 9 + 1/2 * 11 = 4.5 + 5.5 = 10

X.left = 7
X.right = 10
